WHAT IS ANIME?

According to the Japanese concept of anime, this term is used to classify all types from animations, that is, regardless of their origin (national or foreign). The origin of the word anime is uncertain. Some researchers consider it to be an abbreviation of the English animation, while others argue that it may have arisen from the French animée ("animated"). Anime can feature numerous different types such as children's, horror, romance, comedy, adventure, erotic science fiction, etc.

CHARACTERISTICS OF ANIME

Anime cartoons are characterized by the intense demonstration of the feelings and emotions of the characters through their facial expressions. The large eyes, full of brightness and color, the onomatopoeia and exaggerated expressions are some of the most common characteristics in anime.

Mappa

MAPPA Co., Ltd. is a Japanese animation studio founded on June 14, 2011 by Masao Maruyama, founder and former producer of Madhouse. "MAPPA" is an acronym for Maruyama Animation Produce Project Association.

Ufotable

Ufotable is a Japanese animation studio based in Suginami, Tokyo. Hikaru Kondou - founded the studio in October 2000. The company's name originates from a UFO-shaped table that Kondou found while developing the studio's vision.

Pierrot

Pierrot Co., Ltd. is a Japanese animation studio founded in May 1979 by former employees of Tatsunoko Production and Mushi Production. Pierrot is known for several Series of popular anime all over the world.
